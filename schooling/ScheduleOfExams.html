Examination Regulations

Students must strictly adhere to the examination rules. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action or module invalidation.

  • **Attendance:** Students must arrive 15 minutes before the start of the exam. Absence must be justified by an official medical certificate.
  • **Identification:** A valid student card is mandatory for all exams.
  • **Prohibited Items:** Use of mobile phones, smartwatches, and unauthorized documents is strictly forbidden.
  • **Appeals:** Procedures for viewing exam papers and lodging appeals will be communicated with the results.
