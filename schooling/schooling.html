Important Student Information

The Vice Dean's Office for Pedagogy ensures the smooth running of all academic activities. Key services include:

  • **Enrollment Services:** Managing annual re-enrollment and student file updates.
  • **Course Organization:** Allocation of lecture halls, labs, and planning of practical sessions (TD/TP).
  • **Official Announcements:** Publishing official notices regarding academic closures or schedule modifications.
  • **Location:** Administrative block, 1st floor, Office of the Vice Dean.
