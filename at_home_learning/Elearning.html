What is the E-learning Platform?

Our E-learning platform (powered by **Moodle**) provides students and faculty with a robust, centralized environment for digital learning. It complements face-to-face instruction and facilitates **distance education**.

You can access resources for every module in which you are enrolled, including:

  • **Lecture Notes (Cours):** Downloadable PDFs and presentations.
  • **Tutorials (TD) and Practical (TP) Documents:** Exercise sheets and lab instructions.
  • **Quizzes and Assignments:** Online submissions and automated testing.
  • **Forums and Communication:** Direct communication channels with professors and classmates.

Getting Started Guide

  1. **Log In:** Enter your ENT username and password.
  2. **Find Your Course:** Use the search bar or navigate by department (Civil, Mechanical, etc.).
  3. **Enroll:** Some courses require a manual enrollment key, usually provided by your professor.
  4. **Start Learning:** Download materials, participate in forums, and check the calendar for deadlines.
