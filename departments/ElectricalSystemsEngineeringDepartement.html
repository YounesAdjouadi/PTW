Electrical Systems Engineering

Powering the Future: Automation, Telecommunications, and Smart Grids

Department Overview

The Department of Electrical Systems Engineering at the Faculty of Technology is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in all aspects of electrical engineering. Our comprehensive programs cover power systems, electronics, telecommunications, automation, and renewable energy technologies, preparing students for diverse career opportunities in the rapidly evolving electrical engineering field.

With state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty members, we provide students with both theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical experience. Our curriculum is designed to meet industry demands and includes modern topics such as smart grids, IoT systems, 5G communications, and sustainable energy solutions.

The department maintains strong partnerships with leading electrical companies, telecommunications providers, and energy utilities, offering students valuable internship opportunities and exposure to real-world engineering projects. Our research activities focus on cutting-edge areas including renewable energy integration, smart grid technologies, advanced control systems, and next-generation communication networks.
