Mission and Expertise

The Electrical Systems Engineering (ISE) Department trains specialists in the fields of **Electrical Energy**, **Automation**, and **Applied Electronics**. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge education covering everything from the design of smart electrical grids to the programming of embedded industrial control systems.

We prepare graduates for high-demand careers in renewable energy, telecommunications, manufacturing automation, and power distribution infrastructure.

Core Focus Areas

  • **Electrical Power Systems:** Generation, transmission, and distribution optimization.
  • **Industrial Automation & Control:** PLC programming, robotics, and process control.
  • **Electronics & Embedded Systems:** Design of circuits and microcontrollers for smart devices.

Academic Programs

Our programs are structured to offer deep specialization after a solid foundation in core electrical principles:

1. Bachelor's (L3) - Common Core: Electrical Engineering

Covers fundamental concepts including circuit analysis, electromagnetic fields, electrical machines, and introductory programming.

2. Master's (M) Specializations (2 years)

  • **Master in Power Systems and Renewable Energy:** Focus on integrating solar, wind, and smart grid technologies.
  • **Master in Industrial Automation and Computer Science:** Advanced control theories, distributed systems, and industrial networking.
  • **Master in Instrumentation and Applied Electronics:** Design and maintenance of electronic measuring and sensing equipment.

3. Doctorate (D)

Research focused on areas such as advanced robust control, power quality improvement, and optimization of energy storage systems.

Research and Laboratories

The department actively contributes to regional and international research through its highly active laboratories:

  • **Laboratory of Electrical and Industrial Control Systems (LESCI):** Focusing on complex control algorithms and industrial optimization.
  • **Energy and Applied Electronics Research Unit (EREA):** Dedicated to renewable energy integration and power electronics development.
