Mechanical Engineering Department

Driving Innovation in Design, Energy, and Manufacturing

Department Overview

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Faculty of Technology is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in the field of mechanical systems. Our comprehensive programs cover the fundamental principles and advanced applications of mechanical engineering, including design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, materials science, and energy systems.

With state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty members, we prepare our students to become skilled engineers capable of solving complex mechanical engineering challenges. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience, ensuring graduates are well-equipped for successful careers in industry, research, or academia.

The department maintains strong partnerships with leading industrial companies and research institutions, providing students with valuable internship opportunities and exposure to real-world engineering projects. Our research activities focus on cutting-edge areas such as advanced manufacturing, renewable energy systems, robotics, and sustainable design.
