Process Engineering Department

Innovation in Chemical, Environmental, and Pharmaceutical Industries

Department Overview

The Department of Process Engineering is dedicated to educating the next generation of engineers in chemical processes, petroleum refining, and industrial operations. Our comprehensive curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications in state-of-the-art facilities.

Students gain hands-on experience working with industry-standard equipment and software, preparing them for successful careers in chemical, petroleum, and process industries. Our graduates are highly sought after by leading companies in Algeria and internationally.
