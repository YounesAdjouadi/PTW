Mission and Focus

The Civil Engineering Department is dedicated to training professionals capable of tackling complex challenges in construction, transportation, hydraulics, and environmental engineering. We emphasize a foundation in **structural analysis, geotechnical engineering, and sustainable development**.

Our curriculum is designed to combine rigorous theoretical knowledge with extensive practical application through laboratory work and mandatory field internships.
