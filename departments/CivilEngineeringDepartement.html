Civil Engineering Department

Building the Foundations of Tomorrow's Infrastructure

Department Overview

The Department of Civil Engineering at the Faculty of Technology is committed to excellence in education, research, and professional practice in the field of civil infrastructure development. Our comprehensive programs encompass all major areas of civil engineering, including **structural engineering**, **construction management**, **geotechnical engineering**, **transportation**, and **water resources**.

With modern laboratories and experienced faculty members, we prepare our students to become competent civil engineers capable of designing, constructing, and maintaining the infrastructure that supports modern society. Our curriculum emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications, ensuring graduates are ready to tackle real-world engineering challenges.

The department maintains strong collaborations with construction companies, consulting firms, and government agencies, providing students with valuable internship opportunities and exposure to actual construction projects. Our research focuses on **sustainable infrastructure**, **earthquake engineering**, **innovative construction materials**, and **smart city technologies**.
