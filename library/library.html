Specialized Collection Focus

Our collection is tailored to the needs of the technology sector, with strong holdings in:

  • **Applied Mathematics and Physics:** Essential foundation for all engineering disciplines.
  • **Material Science and Nanotechnology:** Resources for advanced research in mechanical and process engineering.
  • **Sustainable Infrastructure:** Focus on green building, renewable energy, and water management.
  • **Automation and Control Systems:** Literature supporting the electrical and mechanical departments.
