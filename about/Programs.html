Doctoral Programs (PhD - LMD)

Excellence in Engineering Research

The Faculty offers Doctoral (PhD) programs across all its engineering disciplines. These programs are designed for highly motivated candidates seeking to contribute original research to their field under the guidance of our distinguished research professors.

Admission is highly competitive and linked to active research projects (PRFU and CNEPRU). Prospective candidates must review current thesis topics and application deadlines via the institutional research portal.
