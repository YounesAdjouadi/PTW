Faculty Organizational Chart

Structure and administration of the Faculty of Technology.

Organizational Hierarchy

The Faculty is structured according to the Algerian university governance model, ensuring clear lines of academic and administrative responsibilities.

Key Administrative Divisions

Vice Dean for Pedagogy

Responsible for all academic matters, student enrollment, course scheduling, and examination management.

  • Academic Affairs Office
  • Student Orientation and Information

Vice Dean for Post-Graduation & Research

Manages Master's and Doctoral programs, research projects (PRFU), scientific publications, and continuous training.

  • Post-Graduation Department
  • Research and Innovation Unit

General Secretariat

Oversees financial resources, human resources, general administration, infrastructure, and facility maintenance.

  • Human Resources Office
  • Budget and Accounting Service

Faculty Council (Conseil de Faculté)

The deliberative body responsible for approving academic strategies, curricula, and institutional policies.

  • Scientific Committee
  • Disciplinary Board
