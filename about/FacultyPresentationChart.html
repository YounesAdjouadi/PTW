Organizational Structure

The Faculty of Technology is governed by a clear structure designed to optimize academic quality, research effectiveness, and administrative efficiency. The chart below illustrates the hierarchy, from the Dean's Office to the different departments and research units.

Visual Organizational Chart

Click to enlarge or download the official organizational document.

Key Components:

  • **Dean's Office (Doyen):** Strategic leadership and representation.
  • **Vice Deans:** Responsible for Pedagogy, Post-Graduation, and Scientific Research.
  • **Departments:** The core academic units (Civil, Mechanical, Process, Electrical).
  • **Administrative Services:** Managing schooling, finance, and human resources.
