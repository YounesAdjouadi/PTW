About the Faculty of Technology

Pioneering the future of engineering education and industrial innovation.

Our Commitment to Excellence

The Faculty of Technology at the University of Boumerdes (UMBB) is recognized as a leader in engineering sciences and applied research in Algeria. Established to meet the growing industrial and infrastructure demands, we are dedicated to providing students with rigorous academic programs, cutting-edge laboratories, and strong industry partnerships.

Our mission goes beyond teaching; we strive to foster a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and ethical practice among our future engineers. Through continuous curriculum evaluation and collaboration with national and international bodies, we ensure our graduates are prepared to tackle the complex challenges of the 21st century.

Explore our programs, meet our distinguished faculty, and discover why the Faculty of Technology is the right choice for your engineering journey.
