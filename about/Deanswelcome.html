Welcome to the Faculty of Technology, a center of innovation and academic excellence.

A Community Dedicated to Technical and Scientific Progress

It is with great pride that I welcome you to the official website of the Faculty of Technology at the University of M'hamed Bougara (UMBB). For decades, our institution has been at the forefront of engineering and applied sciences, training generations of highly competent engineers and researchers who now drive technological progress both nationally and internationally.

**Prof. Dr. Ahmed Benali**
Dean of the Faculty of Technology

Our Commitment to You

We believe in education that goes beyond theory. Our commitment is to provide a dynamic learning environment enriched by **state-of-the-art laboratories**, **active research teams**, and **strong industrial partnerships**. Whether you are a new student starting your academic journey, a seasoned researcher looking for collaboration, or an industry partner seeking innovation, you will find here a vibrant community ready to support your ambitions.

Looking to the Future

The technological landscape is constantly evolving. Our curricula in Civil, Mechanical, Process, and Electrical Systems Engineering are continuously updated to reflect the latest global trends and emerging needs in the job market. Our goal is not just to teach technology, but to foster the **critical thinking** and **problem-solving skills** necessary to invent the future.

I encourage you to explore our departments, research centers, and student services. We are dedicated to your success and look forward to witnessing your contributions to the world of technology.

Best regards,

**Prof. Dr. Ahmed Benali**
*Dean, Faculty of Technology, UMBB*
