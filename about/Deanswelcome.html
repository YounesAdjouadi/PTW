A Word from the Dean

Prof. Mohamed SAIDI shares his vision for the Faculty of Technology.

Welcome to the Faculty of Technology

It is with immense pride and enthusiasm that I welcome you to the Faculty of Technology at the University of Boumerdes. Whether you are a prospective student, a dedicated faculty member, a valued partner, or an alumnus, you are an integral part of our mission for technological excellence.

Our Faculty has always been at the forefront of engineering education in Algeria, committed to providing rigorous, research-led programs across our four specialized departments: Process Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Electrical Systems Engineering. We believe that true engineering mastery lies not just in theoretical knowledge but in practical application and ethical problem-solving.

Our goal is to cultivate not just competent engineers, but visionaries who will lead the next wave of industrial and sustainable development in the region.

Focusing on the Future

We are continuously investing in state-of-the-art laboratory equipment and fostering an environment that encourages interdisciplinary research and innovation. The collaboration between our academic staff and industry partners ensures that our curriculum remains relevant and that our graduates possess the skills demanded by the modern job market.

As we look forward, we are focused on expanding our international footprint and increasing our contribution to major national research projects (PRFU). We invite you to join us in this journey of discovery and impact. Your success is our ultimate measure of achievement.

With warm regards,

Prof. Mohamed SAIDI

Dean of the Faculty of Technology
