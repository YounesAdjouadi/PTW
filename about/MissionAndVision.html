Our core commitments and long-term aspirations for excellence in education and research.

Our Mission: To Educate, Innovate, and Serve

The Faculty of Technology is committed to providing a high-quality educational experience that prepares students to become highly skilled, ethical, and innovative engineers and researchers. Our mission is centered on:

  • **Excellence in Pedagogy:** Delivering current and relevant curricula across all engineering disciplines.
  • **Pioneering Research:** Conducting cutting-edge scientific research that contributes to sustainable technological development and solves complex industrial problems.
  • **Societal Impact:** Serving the community and industry by promoting knowledge transfer and training professionals capable of leading national economic growth.

Our Vision: Global Leadership in Technology

Our long-term vision is to be recognized as a leading technological faculty in the Mediterranean region and beyond. We aim to achieve this by:

  1. **Setting Standards:** Becoming the benchmark institution for engineering education quality in Algeria.
  2. **Expanding Influence:** Broadening our global research collaborations and industry partnerships.
  3. **Fostering Talent:** Creating an environment that attracts and develops world-class faculty and the brightest students.
