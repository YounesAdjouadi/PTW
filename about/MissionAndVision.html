Mission, Vision & Values

Our guiding principles defining the Faculty of Technology's commitment to education and research.

Our Core Mission

The mission of the Faculty of Technology at UMBB is to be a leader in engineering education and scientific research. We are dedicated to:

  • Providing high-quality academic programs that are continuously updated to align with national and international industry standards.
  • Conducting impactful scientific research and technological development that addresses critical societal and industrial challenges in Algeria and beyond.
  • Fostering a strong culture of collaboration and partnership with industry, governmental agencies, and international academic institutions.
  • Developing ethically responsible and highly competent engineers capable of driving innovation and economic growth.

Our Strategic Vision

By 2030, the Faculty of Technology aims to achieve the following strategic goals:

  • Regional Recognition: To be recognized as the foremost Faculty of Technology in the Maghreb region for engineering education and applied research excellence.
  • Global Impact: To significantly increase international collaboration, visibility, and the placement of our graduates in global enterprises.
  • Research Excellence: To establish dedicated research centers focused on emerging technologies (e.g., Renewable Energy, Smart Cities) that attract international funding and talent.

Fundamental Values

Innovation

Encouraging creativity, embracing new technologies, and promoting entrepreneurial spirit among students and faculty.

Integrity & Ethics

Upholding the highest standards of academic honesty, professional integrity, and ethical responsibility in all our activities.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in teaching, learning, and research, ensuring rigorous quality standards across all our programs.

Collaboration

Promoting teamwork, interdisciplinary engagement, and strong ties with industrial and academic partners worldwide.
